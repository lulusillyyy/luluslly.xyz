2025/12/18

Categories: music android computing Tags: phone

Hot-but-not take: Old shitty Android phones are cool actually – they make very good music consumption devices for the unbeatable price of free-ninety-nine (so long as you already have one, of course).

While you can use any modern device of your desire to stream music on the various popular platforms, there is something to be appreciated in a dedicated music player.

Most of us born before the advent of modern streaming services are familiar with the idea of walking around with an MP3 player. It may seem hard to believe, but people used carry around a device separate from their cell phone/pager in order to listen to their favorite albums on the go. (In some cases, phones didn’t even exist at that point.) You could rip your CD collection to your PC and literally drag ’n drop to your chosen digital audio player (DAP).
